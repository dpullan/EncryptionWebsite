Create near unbreakable passwords

To create passwords using this site you will need to enter in your desired password into the first form and a desired key(which could be anything although it should be at least the same length as your password) then using the multiple buttons on the site you can run your password through various phases or encode the 25 most used passwords of 2017 to make them more powerful if you don't wish to encode your own password.

  • Encrypt your plaintext:

    Encrypts your plaintext against your key

  • Decrypt your plaintext:

    Decrypts your plaintext against your key

  • Encrypt and Decrypt your plaintext:

    Encrypts and Decrypts your plaintext against your key

  • Encrypt All:

    Encrypts your plaintext using the 25 most common passowords of 2017 as the keys

  • Encrypt against your key:

    Encrypts the 25 most common passwords of 2017 against your key

  • Create a password:

    Creates a near unbreakable password from your plaintext and key
